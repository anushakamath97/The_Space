Saturn can be seen with the naked eye. It is the fifth brightest object in the solar system and is also easily studied through binoculars or a small telescope. Saturn was known to the ancients, including the Babylonians and Far Eastern observers. Saturn is the flattest planet.Saturn has oval-shaped storms similar to Jupiter’s. Saturn orbits the Sun once every 29.4 Earth years. Saturn’s upper atmosphere is divided into bands of clouds. Saturn is made mostly of hydrogen. Saturn has 150 moons and smaller moonlets. All are frozen worlds. The largest moons are Titan and Rhea. Titan is a moon with complex and dense nitrogen-rich atmosphere. Four spacecraft have visited Saturn.
