Neptune was not known to the ancients. It is not visible to the naked eye and was first observed in 1846. Neptune spins on its axis very rapidly. Neptune is the smallest of the ice giants. The atmosphere of Neptune is made of hydrogen and helium, with some methane. Neptune has a very active climate.One of the largest storms ever seen was recorded in 1989. It was called the Great Dark Spot. It lasted about five years. Neptune has 14 moons. Neptune has a very thin collection of rings.
